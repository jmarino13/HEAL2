Privacy Policy & HIPAA Notice

Last Updated:

This Privacy Policy describes how HEAL Wellness (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and protects information from individuals who visit our website, complete our medical intake forms, participate in telehealth visits, purchase services, or otherwise interact with us (“Consumers” or “Patients”).

By accessing or using our website or services, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. This Privacy Policy is intended to work together with our HIPAA Notice of Privacy Practices.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal & Contact Information

We may collect information that you choose to provide, including:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Date of birth
  • Mailing address and state of residence
  • Preferred contact method and membership preferences

b. Medical & Health Information (PHI)

Through our medical history questionnaire, telehealth visits, and ongoing care, we may collect health-related information such as:

  • Medical history, diagnoses, and conditions
  • Current and past medications and supplements
  • Allergies and prior reactions
  • Surgical history and hospitalizations
  • Laboratory results and vital signs, if applicable
  • Information related to your use of GLP-1 medications, peptide therapy, hormones, and estriol-based creams

This health information may be considered Protected Health Information (“PHI”) under the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act of 1996 (“HIPAA”).

c. Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect:

  • IP address
  • Browser and device type
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • General location information (non-GPS)

d. Cookies & Similar Technologies

We may use cookies and similar technologies to support website functionality, remember your preferences, and understand how visitors use our site. You can adjust cookie settings within your browser; however, disabling cookies may affect certain site features.

e. Text Messaging & Marketing Communications

By providing your phone number, you agree that HEAL Wellness may contact you via text message regarding appointments, treatment updates, and limited promotional information. Message & data rates may apply. Message frequency may vary. You may reply STOP or UNSUBSCRIBE at any time to opt out, or reply HELP for more information.

2. How We Use Your Information

a. Providing Medical & Wellness Services

We use your information to:

  • Review your medical intake and determine eligibility for services
  • Deliver GLP-1, peptide, hormone, and skincare treatments
  • Coordinate telehealth visits and follow-up care
  • Manage memberships, billing, and renewals

b. Communications

We may use your contact information to:

  • Send appointment confirmations and reminders
  • Provide important updates related to your treatment plan
  • Share educational materials and wellness resources
  • Send limited marketing and promotional communications (which you may opt out of)

c. Website Operation & Improvement

We may use aggregated and de-identified information to monitor site performance, analyze usage trends, and improve our services and user experience.

3. HIPAA & Protected Health Information (PHI)

a. HIPAA Compliance

When we create, receive, maintain, or transmit PHI in our role as a healthcare provider, we do so in accordance with HIPAA and applicable state privacy laws. We implement administrative, physical, and technical safeguards designed to protect the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of your PHI.

b. Uses & Disclosures of PHI

We may use and disclose your PHI for purposes of treatment, payment, and healthcare operations (“TPO”), including:

  • Treatment: Sharing information with clinicians, pharmacies, laboratories, or other healthcare professionals involved in your care.
  • Payment: Using PHI to bill for services, process payments, or work with third-party payment processors.
  • Healthcare Operations: Quality assessment, training, credentialing, and other activities necessary to operate our practice.

We may also use or disclose PHI when permitted or required by law, such as:

  • To comply with court orders or legal processes
  • To report suspected abuse, neglect, or certain injuries
  • To public health authorities as required
  • To prevent or lessen a serious and imminent threat to health or safety

c. HIPAA Notice of Privacy Practices

This web-based Privacy Policy is intended as a general explanation of our privacy practices. Our full HIPAA Notice of Privacy Practices (“NPP”) provides more detailed information about how we may use and disclose your PHI and your rights under HIPAA. You may request a copy of our NPP at any time by contacting us using the information below.

d. Your HIPAA Rights

Under HIPAA, you may have rights to:

  • Request access to your medical records and PHI
  • Request amendments to your PHI if you believe it is inaccurate or incomplete
  • Request restrictions on certain uses and disclosures of your PHI
  • Request an accounting of certain disclosures
  • Request communications by alternative means or at alternative locations
  • File a complaint with us or with the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services

We will not retaliate against you for filing a complaint or exercising your privacy rights.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your Personally Identifiable Information or PHI to third parties for marketing purposes.

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

a. Service Providers & Business Associates

We may share information with third-party service providers and HIPAA Business Associates that perform functions on our behalf, such as:

  • Telehealth platforms
  • Pharmacies and fulfillment partners
  • Payment processors
  • Secure hosting and electronic medical record vendors
  • IT support and security providers

These parties are required to use appropriate safeguards and may only use your information as necessary to perform services for HEAL Wellness.

b. Legal & Regulatory Requirements

We may disclose information when required to do so by law, regulation, court order, or government request, or when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.

c. Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, reorganization, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity, subject to this Privacy Policy and applicable laws.

5. Your Choices & Rights (Non-HIPAA)

In addition to your HIPAA rights, you may:

  • Opt out of marketing emails by using the “unsubscribe” link in any email
  • Opt out of SMS marketing by replying STOP or UNSUBSCRIBE
  • Request access, correction, or deletion of certain personal information, subject to legal and medical retention requirements

6. Data Security

We use a combination of administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information, including encryption, access controls, and secure hosting environments. While we strive to use commercially reasonable means to protect your data, no system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our services are intended for adults and, where appropriate, older adolescents under the supervision of a parent or legal guardian. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without appropriate consent.
