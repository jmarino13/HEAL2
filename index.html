FAQ

Quick answers before you begin.

How do you determine if I qualify?

Eligibility is based on your medical history, current medications, body measurements, and state-specific regulations. Our clinicians review everything before prescribing any GLP-1, peptide, hormone, or topical therapy.

Which states do you serve?

We’re continuously expanding. When you enter your state and complete the form, we’ll let you know if HEAL Wellness is currently available there and which services are offered.

Will I speak with a real person?

Yes. A licensed provider reviews every intake, and in many cases you’ll have a live telehealth visit to review your plan, lab work, and questions before starting.

Are memberships required?

No – but most patients prefer 3, 6, or 12-month memberships for better pricing and continuity. We’ll help you choose the right option once we understand your goals.
